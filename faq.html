Frequently Asked Questions

📍 Where is BrightWave Hostel located?

We are located in Osunlade Village, Malete, Kwara State — just minutes from the university area.

🔐 What security measures are in place?

24/7 security personnel, CCTV surveillance, and gated entrances with restricted access.

💧 Is water supply available all the time?

Yes. We offer 24/7 water supply supported by a borehole and backup water tank system.

⚡ What happens during power outages?

We run on solar power, and we also have a general generator to support all rooms when needed.

🏠 Can I book a hostel room in advance?

Yes, contact us via the form on our hostel detail page or contact page to reserve your space.

🛠 Is Phase 1 open now?

Phase 1 is under active construction and scheduled to open soon. Bookings will be available once completed.
