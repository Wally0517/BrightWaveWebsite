Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for students, land buyers, and investors in Nigeria

Join the waitlist on the hostel detail page or message us on WhatsApp. We’ll confirm availability, share requirements (valid ID, admission letter or student ID), and guide you through payment and allocation.
Rooms are self-contained with water supply included. Power is supported with solar backup and a general generator for common services. Tenants fund their personal electricity usage (where applicable). Routine maintenance of common areas is included.
Security includes gated access, trained personnel and CCTV around key points. Visitor and quiet-hours policies are communicated during onboarding to keep everyone safe and productive.
Internet options vary by phase and provider. Many students use personal MiFi/routers. Confirm current Wi-Fi availability with our team during booking.
Yes—select sites allow installment plans. Typical structure: 20–30% initial deposit, then monthly or quarterly spreads (3–12 months depending on the estate). Exact plans are shared with the offer letter for that location.
Standard pack includes a Receipt/Invoice, Deed of Assignment, Registered Survey (or Survey in process), Layout plan/allocation letter, and estate covenants where applicable. Title type differs by site—our team will state whether it’s C of O, R of O, customary consent, or in-process.
We encourage site inspections and independent checks. You can verify survey details with the Surveyor-General’s office and confirm title at the Lands Registry. We also provide beacons/coordinates and support due diligence.
Possible extras (estate-dependent): survey fee, deed preparation/registration, development levy, and allocation/utility fees. We provide a transparent fee sheet before payment.
After completing agreed payments and documentation, allocation is scheduled (often within 2–6 weeks, estate-specific). You’ll receive your plot beaconing and can take possession accordingly.
We consider joint ventures (land-for-equity), build-to-rent financing, and bulk hostel blocks. Share your proposal via email and our team will schedule a session to review scope, security, timelines, and returns.
Yields vary by location, room mix and occupancy. Student accommodations in strong university markets commonly target competitive cash yields; we share pro-formas and sensitivity analysis during due diligence.
Bank transfer is preferred for auditability. Official account details are provided on your invoice/offer letter only—always confirm with our team before sending funds.
Refunds follow the terms on your offer letter/tenancy. Administrative and processing fees may apply. For land, refunds are subject to the stage of processing (survey/deed) and any outstanding deductions.
